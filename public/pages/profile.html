The Weaver's Narrative

For Kemi Adeyemi, weaving is more than just a craft; it is a conversation with the ancestors. Born in the heart of Yorubaland, she began learning the intricacies of the Aso Oke loom at the age of seven, guided by her grandmother's weathered but steady hands.

Her work today blends ancient geometric patterns with modern, minimalist aesthetics. Each piece takes weeks of meticulous effort, using hand-dyed organic cotton and silk threads that capture the vibrant spirit of contemporary Lagos while honoring the weight of tradition.
